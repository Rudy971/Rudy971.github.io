Meet The Artist

About Me

Hi! My name is Tiffany. Drawing is kinda my thing. If you want to know how to spot me out and about, I'm the one with the big frizzy hair, a pen in one hand  and a sketchbook in the other. I've always got at least one to hand. If something comes to mind, Inset it down on my page. I have what many have named an “overactive imagination” so these sketches can become pretty original at times. These imagination sketches began to develop into a portfolio of kinds and thus came, Imagine Illustration.

I study Film and TV production. But I'm constantly thinking of Art. Since everyone keeps talking about me having “a future” and finding a “career” it seemed only natural to make Imagine Illustration my future. After all, aside from binge-watching Criminal Minds and scrolling through Pinterest for unhealthy lengths of time, Illustration is how I enjoy spending my time the most. And what's better than doing what you love full-time AND earning money from it? Take that parents that are trying to make me get a job and move out.
